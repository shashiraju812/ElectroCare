# ⚡ ElectroCare - Smart Electrical Shopping Mobile App

## 📱 Overview

ElectroCare is a Flutter-based mobile application designed to provide a seamless and efficient platform for purchasing electrical products. The app enables users to browse products, view detailed specifications, manage shopping carts, and place orders through a modern and user-friendly mobile interface.

The goal of ElectroCare is to digitize electrical retail shopping by offering customers a convenient and secure mobile shopping experience while helping businesses expand their reach through digital transformation.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

* Simplify the purchase of electrical products.
* Provide a user-friendly mobile shopping experience.
* Enable efficient product browsing and searching.
* Offer secure and reliable order management.
* Demonstrate modern mobile application development using Flutter.

## 🔄 Application Workflow

1. User creates an account or logs in.
2. Browse available electrical products.
3. Search products by category or name.
4. View detailed product information.
5. Add products to shopping cart.
6. Place an order.
7. Track order status within the application.
